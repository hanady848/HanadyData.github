Business Request & User Stories:

You are a data analyst. For the next quarter, your company will be running a marketing campaign. The marketing manager wants to target those segments that have donated the most in the past year. She turned to you to help her with her upcoming meeting with the CEO.

Data Source:

You have access to the following information:
• "category" - "Sports", "Fashion", "Technology", etc.
• "device" - the type of device used.
• "gender" - gender of the user.
• "age range" - one of five age brackets.
• "amount" - how much the user donated in Euros.

Clean and Prepare the Data:

1 - Create A Date table using DAX.
2-Make age grouping After Checking Min and Max age in the Data
Less than 20
20-30
30-40
40-50
More than 50
3-Create Measure Table to put all our measure/calculated column inside.
4-Create income grouping after checking Min and Max income in the data.

Questions to answer:

1- Which camp was most successful?
2- How many customers does the company have... and how many customers I gain in the year?
3- Did I lose any customer / year? For how long they didn not purchase from the company?
4- What about customers complain about our products??
5- Which ages of customers buy more from the company??
6- If the customer has kids is this affect how much they purchase from the company??
7- Is the marital status of the customer affected his purchasing?
8- Is the income from the customer affected his purchasing?
9- Which product the companies sell more??
10- How customers react to the Campaign?
11- Which have more purchase order (store / website)?
